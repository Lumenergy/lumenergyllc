About

Lumenergy LLC is a single‑member consultancy owned and operated by Maia Leroy. Maia has over three years of experience in shaping climate and energy policy in California and an academic background in physics with six years of laboratory and analytical experience. She combines critical thinking and compassionate advocacy to push towards a cleaner, more equitable energy future. She has authored influential reports, testified before legislative committees, and led successful coalitions to advance renewable energy and transmission solutions. Work is scoped to your team’s needs and capacity and can be conducted on an hourly, project, or retainer basis.
